Everything you need to know about Route 39

Route39 is an entirely electric auto service with zero cancellations, no surge pricing, and premium rides at local fares. We prioritize safety, sustainability, and customer convenience with features like GPS tracking, verified drivers, and emergency support.

Active drivers earn between ₹20,000–40,000 per month depending on hours worked and city location. Earnings include base fare, distance charges, and surge pricing during peak hours. All payments are processed weekly with transparent fare breakdowns.

Download the Route39 app, create your account, enter your destination, and confirm your pickup location. You'll see the fare estimate before booking. Track your driver in real-time and pay securely through the app.

Our entire fleet is electric, producing zero emissions. Every ride with Route39 contributes to reducing air pollution and carbon footprint, making transportation sustainable for future generations.

You need a valid driving license (minimum 3 years), clean driving record, Aadhaar card, and police verification. Free training is provided. No vehicle ownership required.

We offer real-time GPS tracking, safety snapshots with face recognition, verified drivers with visible IDs, SOS button, hygiene-compliant vehicles, and 24/7 emergency support to ensure your safety at all times.

We currently operate in Tiruppur and Coimbatore. We are expanding our services to more locations soon. Stay tuned!
